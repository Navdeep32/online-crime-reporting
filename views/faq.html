FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

What is the purpose of the online crime reporting website?

The online crime reporting website is a platform designed to allow users to report non-emergency crimes or incidents online, providing a convenient and efficient way to communicate with law enforcement.

What types of incidents can be reported online?

You can use the online reporting system for non-emergency incidents such as vandalism, theft, lost property, harassing phone calls, and other minor crimes.

When should I use the online reporting system?

The online reporting system is ideal for incidents that do not require immediate police attention. If it's an emergency or if a crime is in progress, please call 911 or your local emergency number.

How do I submit an online crime report?

To submit a report, navigate to the "Report a Crime" section on our website and follow the step-by-step instructions. Provide accurate and detailed information about the incident.

What information should I include in my online report?

Include as much information as possible, such as the date, time, location, description of the incident, and any relevant details about the individuals involved. Attach photos or documents if available.

Is my personal information secure when using the online reporting system?

We take your privacy seriously. Your personal information is stored securely, and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

What should I do if I made an error in my online report?

If you made a mistake or need to update your report, please contact our support team at [crimeReports@gmail.com] with your reference number.
